Optimizing operational workflow

Analyzed existing operational workflows and mapped AS-IS processes to identify inefficiencies.

Role Senior Business Analyst
Duration 5 Months
Scope Custody Operations

The Problem

The existing operational workflow lacked a clearly defined process structure, leading to inefficiencies in how teams handled operational activities. Multiple teams relied on manual interventions and fragmented process steps, which created delays, inconsistencies, and operational bottlenecks.

Additionally, there was limited visibility into the AS-IS process flow, making it difficult for stakeholders to identify process gaps, inefficiencies, and opportunities for improvement.

What I Did

My Role:

  • Analyzed the existing AS-IS operational workflow, identified inefficiencies, and collaborated with operations and technology teams to redesign the TO-BE process flow, enabling a more structured and efficient operational process.

Key Decisions

🎯 Defining the AS-IS Process

Map the existing operational workflow to identify inefficiencies and gaps.

🔄 Prioritizing Process Improvements

Focus on redesigning the most critical workflow steps that were causing operational delays.

🤝 Understand the Ops Pain Point

Sat with Operation Team and Their feedback shaped every sprint.

📊 Integrating Process Improvements into Agile Delivery

Convert process improvements into actionable backlog items and support sprint planning.

Results

✅

Operational Efficiency

The redesigned workflow reduced operational ambiguity and improved how teams executed daily processes.

🌍

Better Process Visibility

Clear AS-IS and TO-BE process documentation gave stakeholders a better understanding of operational flows and responsibilities.

⚡

Stronger Agile Execution

Process improvements were converted into backlog items, enabling structured delivery through sprint planning and Agile cycles.

What I Learned

  • Stakeholder Collaboration is Essential
  • Small Improvements Can Create Big Impact
  • Documentation Improves Operational Clarity
