Optimizing operational workflow

Analyzed existing operational workflows and mapped AS-IS processes to identify inefficiencies.

Role Senior Business Analyst
Duration 5 Months
Scope Custody Operations

The Problem

The existing operational workflow lacked a clearly defined process structure, leading to inefficiencies in how teams handled operational activities. Multiple teams relied on manual interventions and fragmented process steps, which created delays, inconsistencies, and operational bottlenecks.

Additionally, there was limited visibility into the AS-IS process flow, making it difficult for stakeholders to identify process gaps, inefficiencies, and opportunities for improvement.

What I Did

My Role:

  • Discovery: Analyzed the existing AS-IS operational workflow, identified inefficiencies, and collaborated with operations and technology teams to redesign the TO-BE process flow, enabling a more structured and efficient operational process.

Key Decisions

🎯 Start with Compliance

MiFID II wasn't optional. I made regulatory requirements the foundation, not an afterthought.

🔄 Iterate Fast

Shipped MVP to one market first, learned, then expanded globally. Reduced risk, increased confidence.

🤝 Embed with Users

Sat with settlement and ops teams weekly. Their feedback shaped every sprint.

📊 Instrument Everything

Built reporting into the product from day one. Made compliance audits painless.

Results

✅

Full Compliance

Passed MiFID II audits with zero issues

🌍

Global Rollout

Deployed across multiple markets

⚡

Operational Efficiency

Automated what was previously manual reconciliation

What I Learned

  • Compliance products can still be user-friendly — it's a design choice
  • The best specs come from sitting with users, not reading documents
  • In regulated industries, "move fast and break things" doesn't work — but "move deliberately and ship often" does
