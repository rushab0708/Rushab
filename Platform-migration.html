Application Migration

Migration of legacy application

Role Senior Project Analyst
Duration 2 Years
Scope Custody Operations

The Problem

The legacy STARS Reconciliation application was being decommissioned, requiring the migration of multiple clients/accounts and reconciliation processes to the FIS Intellimatch platform.

The challenge was to ensure a seamless transition while preserving business-critical reconciliation functionality, reporting requirements, and data integrity.

What I Did

My Role:

  • Worked closely with Onshore stakeholders, Business users, and teams to gather and analyze business requirements.

    • Documented requirements through BRDs, FRDs, process flows, and data mapping specifications. Coordinated migration activities, supported user acceptance testing (UAT), and facilitated communication between business and technical teams throughout the transition lifecycle.

Key Decisions

🎯Defined Data Mapping Strategy

Established data mapping rules between the legacy STARS application and the Intellimatch platform by collaborating with business and technical stakeholders.

🔄Prioritized Client Migration Requirements

Worked with stakeholders to identify and prioritize critical business requirements, reports, and reconciliation workflows during the migration

🤝 Introduced Structured UAT Approach

Defined test scenarios, acceptance criteria, and coordinated User Acceptance Testing activities across business users and project teams.

📊 Conducted GAP Analysis for Process Alignment

Analyzed differences between legacy and target-state processes and recommended solutions to address functional gaps.

Results

✅

Successful Platform Migration

Enabled business continuity while decommissioning the legacy platform.

🌍

Improved Requirement Traceability

Reduced ambiguity and improved alignment between business and technology teams.

⚡

Effective Stakeholder Collaboration

Ensured timely delivery and increased stakeholder confidence in the migration program.

What I Learned

  • Large-Scale Application Migration
  • Data Analysis and Mapping
  • Product Ownership Mindset
